App Instructions

  1. Browse & Study: Tap a character card to view its pronunciation, meaning, example sentence, and related compound word.
  2. Mark as Learned or Keep: Use the “Gotcha” and “Keep” buttons to track your learning progress. Learned cards will be removed from the study stack.
  3. Compound Words: Each card may show a common compound word, its pinyin, and meaning to help you expand your vocabulary.
  4. Example Sentences: Sentences are provided with both Chinese and English, with key words highlighted for context learning.
  5. Search & Library: Use the search and library features to look up any character or word in the app’s database.
  6. Settings: Switch between day/night mode and reset your learning progress at any time.
  7. All progress is saved locally on your device.
